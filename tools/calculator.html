Professional Accessible Standard Calculator

The Accessible Standard Calculator is a high-performance, browser-based tool meticulously engineered for blind and low-vision users. Developed by Accessible Tech Labs India, this utility prioritizes keyboard-centric navigation and seamless screen reader compatibility (NVDA, JAWS, TalkBack). Featuring a high-contrast interface with glassmorphism aesthetics, it ensures maximum legibility for users with visual impairments. Every interaction is optimized with ARIA live regions to provide real-time auditory feedback of inputs and results. Unlike standard calculators, this tool eliminates complex shortcuts in favor of a clear, structured button layout, making mathematical operations intuitive and stress-free. Designed with a Privacy-First approach, all calculations are processed locally within your browser, ensuring no data ever leaves your device. This professional utility is part of our mission to bridge the digital divide through inclusive and accessible software solutions.
