Global OSINT Breach Radar

Accessible Tech Labs India introduces the Global OSINT Breach Radar. In the modern landscape of persistent cyber threats, identifying compromised credentials is the primary defense against unauthorized access. This forensic workstation executes rapid intelligence sweeps across vast, open-source dark web databases and known breach repositories to verify the integrity of any given email address.

Adhering strictly to our Zero-Knowledge protocol, this engine functions exclusively as a secure query bridge. Target email addresses are transmitted securely to external threat-intelligence endpoints, and the localized memory buffer is purged immediately upon generating the forensic report. We do not track, log, or store your queries.

If a compromise is detected, the Ultimate Hybrid engine cross-references the breach signature with global master catalogs to extract the precise nature of the compromised data directly to your workstation, while offering a secure pathway to verify full external reports.
