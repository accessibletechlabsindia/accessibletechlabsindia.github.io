The Privacy Manifesto & System Architecture

At Accessible Tech Labs India, we believe that true digital freedom begins with absolute data sovereignty. This ATL Sovereign Share engine is architected upon a strict zero-knowledge, peer-to-peer (P2P) framework utilizing advanced Web Real-Time Communication (WebRTC) protocols. Unlike conventional cloud-based file sharing platforms that upload your sensitive documents to remote servers, our system establishes a direct, encrypted tunnel between the sending and receiving devices. This means your data never rests on any external database, server, or intermediate storage node. We implement robust Datagram Transport Layer Security (DTLS) and Secure Real-time Transport Protocol (SRTP) to provide end-to-end encryption, ensuring that even if the local network is compromised or intercepted, the transmitted data remains an indecipherable stream of cryptographic binary. Furthermore, by bypassing intermediary servers, we completely eliminate arbitrary bandwidth restrictions and gigabyte limits, allowing for the seamless transfer of massive, uncompressed files and complex folder directories directly at the maximum speed of your local network or Wi-Fi connection. The unique 'atli' session tokens generated for the handshake process are ephemeral and self-destruct immediately upon the conclusion of the transfer or if the session is interrupted, guaranteeing that unauthorized reconnections are mathematically impossible. We have integrated native Web Audio API telemetry and comprehensive ARIA live region support to provide an unparalleled, fully accessible experience for screen reader users, ensuring that security and usability operate in perfect harmony. Empowering you to transfer without limits, without surveillance, and without compromise. Innovation driven by integrity.
