Privacy Policy – Media Composer Engine

Media Composer Engine by Accessible Tech Labs India is a fully offline media processing application designed for professional and accessible audio-video conversion and enhancement. Your privacy and system security are our highest priorities.

This application operates entirely on your local device. All media processing features including Audio to Video conversion, Video to Audio extraction, Add Music to Audio, and Add Music to Video are performed locally without uploading, transmitting, or sharing your files to any external server.

Media Composer Engine does not collect personal information, browsing history, media content, usage analytics, or device identifiers. No background tracking systems or third-party data services are integrated into this application.

All selected audio and video files remain on your system. The application only reads and processes files that you manually choose. Generated output files are saved directly to your selected folder or default system location.

The software does not require user accounts, login credentials, or internet connectivity for core features. Internet access may only be required if future updates are manually checked by the user.

We are committed to accessibility. Media Composer Engine is designed to function smoothly with screen readers and keyboard navigation, ensuring an inclusive and professional user experience.

We recommend downloading installer packages only from our official GitHub release channel or our official website to ensure authenticity and security.
