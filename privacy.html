Privacy Policy

Accessible Tech Labs India respects your privacy. This website is designed to provide accessible document and utility tools without collecting personal information or tracking user activity.

Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. There are no user accounts, login systems, or registration forms on this website.

File Processing

All tools on this platform work directly inside your web browser. Uploaded files such as PDFs, images, or text documents are processed locally on your device. These files are never sent to any server owned or controlled by Accessible Tech Labs India.

Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies, tracking scripts, analytics tools, or advertising services. Your activity is not monitored or recorded.

Third Party Libraries

Some tools use open-source JavaScript libraries to perform document processing in your browser. These libraries operate locally and do not transmit your data externally.

Data Security

Because all processing happens on your own device, you remain in full control of your files. We recommend using trusted devices and keeping your system updated for additional safety.

External Links

This website may contain links to external platforms such as YouTube or Telegram. Accessible Tech Labs India is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites.

Accessibility Commitment

Accessible Tech Labs India is built with accessibility as a core priority. All pages aim to support screen readers, keyboard navigation, and simple layouts to help blind and beginner users use the tools comfortably.
