About Accessible Tech Labs India

Accessible Tech Labs India is an independent technology initiative created by a blind developer with a strong focus on accessibility, simplicity, and practical software development. This platform exists to build tools that are usable by everyone, especially screen reader users and beginners in technology. Every project is carefully designed to work smoothly with keyboard navigation and assistive technologies.

The goal of Accessible Tech Labs India is to provide offline utilities, system tools, and learning resources that help users understand technology in a simple and inclusive way. Alongside software development, this platform also supports ethical cybersecurity learning and encourages responsible use of technology.

This journey started with a simple idea: technology should be accessible to all. Today, Accessible Tech Labs India continues to grow with new tools, improvements, and future plans focused on empowering users through inclusive development.

Mission

To build accessible, reliable, and privacy-friendly technology tools that help users learn, grow, and work independently.
